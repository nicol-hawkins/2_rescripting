My Kickstart Coding Journey has begun!

Oct. 27, 2019

A week ago I started my official coding journey at Kickstart Coding.
The past few months I have been teaching myself to code via online tutorials.
I've learned a lot from them, but I feel they can only take you so far.
Just one week in and I feel like having the guidance of an instructor has
helped me really understand concepts I only had vague notions of before.
Being able to apply these concepts to build something of your own
is super rewarding. This is my first website I've ever built, and I
I'm proud of it! At first I tried to use a template, but decided there was
too much noise and wanted to try from scratch.
